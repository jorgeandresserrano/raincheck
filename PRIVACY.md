# RainCheck Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

RainCheck helps users decide whether it is worth washing their car based on local rain forecasts.

## Information Used

RainCheck may use:

- Approximate device location, when the user grants location permission.
- Manually entered city or place searches.
- Forecast preference settings, such as horizon and rain tolerance.

RainCheck does not require an account.

## How Information Is Used

Location or city information is used to request local weather and geocoding data so the app can show a wash-or-wait recommendation.

Preferences are stored on the device to keep the app configured between launches.

## Third-Party Services

RainCheck sends forecast and location lookup requests to external weather and geocoding services. These requests may include coordinates or a city search query.

## Data Retention

RainCheck stores preferences locally on the device. It does not operate a RainCheck account system or a RainCheck backend database.

## User Choices

Users can:

- Enter a city manually instead of granting location permission.
- Change or revoke location permission in system settings.
- Clear app data by uninstalling the app or using operating system storage controls.
